	for his army, entrusting the command to the former leader of 7th Airborne unit, Storm Eagle~\cite{wayback:X_resources}.

	\item \hypertarget{vehicle:Ride_Armor}{\textbf{Ride Armor:}}
	Ride Armors are mechas similar to tanks with attached hands and feet. Originally these machines were made intended to be used in engineering~\cite{wayback:X_resources}, but were later used also for fighting, as they greatly increase the power of their user due being able to dash, walk over spikes, deliver powerful attacks and receive damage in place of 
	their pilot. Starting from this base version many others will be created, focused more on combat power. Vile himself uses two modified Ride Armors in his confrontation with X, although both of them were destroyed by Zero.

	\item \hypertarget{vehicle:Ride_Armor_Raiden}{\textbf{Ride Armor ``\textit{Raiden}'':}} 
	 Ride Armor developed by the Repliforce's scientists from the Weapons Development Division's,  who felt that the combat efficiency of the 
	former ride armor series were too limited, as they were designed for engineering jobs. Thus  they set out to create the first ride armor designed purely for battle. The Raiden was originally an experimental model, but due to its excellent record in simulations, it was chosen to be mass-produced. Equipped with a beam blade on its forearm for punching and slashing which can also be charged up, it also possesses a jet booster on its back for jumping and dashing. It is ferocious at hand-to-hand combat, but the blade can also be used to destroy some obstacles, and its tough armoring protects the rider from extreme heat or 
	spikes~\cite{wayback:X4_resources}. This powerful ride armor is the most common among Repliforce soldiers, and due its excellent performance, many of them were kept in service far beyond their service time~\cite{book:Compendium}.
	
	\item \hypertarget{vehicle:Ride_Armor_Eagle}{\textbf{Ride Armor ``\textit{Eagle}'':}} 
	The second ride armor prototype develop by Repliforce, this one also was selected due to its high simulation records.  Designed to be specialized in flight and aerial combat, it can hover almost indefinitely, and can air dash as well. It is equipped with a powerful beam cannon which, can also fire 3 guided energy missiles. While charging it cannot walk, but it can still move in the air~\cite{wayback:X4_resources}. 
	
	\item \hypertarget{vehicle:Ride_Chaser_Cheval}{\textbf{Ride Chaser ADU-T400 turbo ``\textit{Cheval}'': }}
	Reploid private air bike. Designed for running at extreme speeds, 
	it is a super advanced vehicle with a useful Turbo function. Weak to impact, due to a fault it instantly self-destruct upon striking on something~\cite{wayback:X2_resources}.

	\item \hypertarget{vehicle:Ride_Chaser_Aidion}{\textbf{Ride Chaser ``Adion'': }}
	The successor of Cheval Ride Chaser. Developed by Dr. Cain, he has fully tuned this model to maximize increase speed and power. Designed for the exclusive use of the Maverick Hunters, but the same technology used to design them was also used by Repliforce, to develop their own version, the ``Hornet''.
	This model is equipped with a canon to fire energy bullets and a drive blade on the underside  which can activate for a wheelie attack, while the propulsion can also be used to destroy attackers from the rear. Its speed has been vastly increased over the previous model, even excessively, as the higher speed expose raiders to greater danger, and only the most skilled Hunters can ride the air bike successfully~\cite{wayback:X4_resources}.
	
	\item \hypertarget{vehicle:Ride_Chaser_Hornet}{\textbf{Ride Chaser ``Hornet'': }}\label{ve}
	The ride chaser model dispatched to the Repliforce Navy, created by illegally modifying~\cite{book:Compendium} (in other source, the schematics were offered~\cite{wayback:X4_resources}) the Adion. Its similarly to the Adion 
	in terms of efficiency, but powered down to make it easier for the Repliforce Naval troops to handle~\cite{wayback:X4_resources}.
